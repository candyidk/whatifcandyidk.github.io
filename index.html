Story

Message

The film's message is going to be about how one's words can affect someone's life so significantly and that people should not take some things lightly.

Setting-Burton High School

The story starts off with Katrina Ikeda, a junior in high school waking up and walking to school. The camera follows her going through the school and faces verbal bullying from people in the halls. People call her fat, a slut, and other different names. In the middle of the story, school ends and she goes back home, forcing herself to throw up because she believes she is fat. The same cycle goes again when she goes back to school, except this time when she goes back home, she doesn't force herself to throw up. Katrina slits her wrists and killls herself. But at the end, time "turns back" or rewinds. Katrina goes to school and does not face the bullying, but rather having people compliment her, preventing her suicide.
